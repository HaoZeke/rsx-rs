* Code of Conduct

The ~rsx~ project adopts the
[[https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/][Contributor
Covenant]] (v2.1). All participants in the project's spaces -- issues, pull
requests, and discussions -- are expected to uphold it.

In short: be respectful and constructive, assume good faith, and keep the
project a welcoming place for contributors of every background. Harassment and
exclusionary behaviour are not tolerated.

The full text and the contact for reporting concerns are in
[[https://github.com/HaoZeke/rsx-rs/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md][CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md]].
Contribution guidelines are in
[[https://github.com/HaoZeke/rsx-rs/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md][CONTRIBUTING.md]].
